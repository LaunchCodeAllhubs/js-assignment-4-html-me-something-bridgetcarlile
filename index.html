Background

Josh and I have been dating since the end of 2020. We talked about getting engaged and picked out the ring together, but he wanted to surprise me with the plans for the engagement. He planned a trip to Spain and France since I studied French in school but have never had the opportunity to visit. He couldn't keep the secret though, so he ended up proposing right after the ring was ready to be picked up. But he already planned the whole thing, so of course we still went and made it a celebration trip.
